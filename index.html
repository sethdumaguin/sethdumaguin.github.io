About Me

Hello! I'm Seth Dumaguin. I am a second year Intended Computer Science major and Geographic Information System Minor. I am an aspiring web developer and software engineer. My purpose of being a computer science major is to combine computer science and teaching together. I love teaching students and making a little difference in their life and that is why my focus on most of my games are teaching games. I mostly create mathematics games because I love math and when I was young I had a hard time practicing on math so I want to help students to not struggle in math in their early years.

CS Projects

Cal-U-Find-It


A game created during CalHacks with my CS partner, Seth Lu. The game is geared towards elementary and middle school students wanting to learn famous landmarks around the state of California. We used JavaScript, CSS, and HTML to create the game. We also used the leaflet API for the map backgroup and different functions. (2017)

Trig of Pearl Harbor


A math game set during the attack on Pearl Harbor and the player are asked to select the correct Trig Identity in the form of care packages. This game enforces quick thinking for players. This game was programmed in HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. This game is a Globaloria Globey Winner. (2016)

Test Your Skills


A math game that lets the player choose between Algebra and Calculus Players are then presented with questions based on the topic that they picked. Players are chased with enemies and they are trying to collect the correct answer before the enemy catches them. This game is a Globaloria Globey Winner(2015)
